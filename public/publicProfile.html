A DJ serves as the master of ceremonies and music box for events such as parties, weddings, and bar trivia nights. Though our participants fit into one demographic, the position can be held by nearly anyone with a knack for music and decent soft skills. They need to know how to mix and blend songs seamlessly throughout an event and must be able to read the crowd and adjust their performance accordingly. It’s important to recognize when the crowd is interested in dancing and when they want to move on to other things. A DJ’s main goal is to have the overall process from setup to takedown be smooth and seamless. The DJ wants the music to fit the event and the vibe of the crowd without technical difficulties or crowd complaints.
